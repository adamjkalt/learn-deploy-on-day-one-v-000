Biography

Originally from Westchester County, New York, I graduated from Boston College in 2005, and relocated to Los Angeles, CA. I spent over 10 years in Los Angeles in the Fitness Industry, working in managerial roles in various gyms, and eventually starting up my own Outdoor Fitness Business in 2008, which then became an Indoor Training Studio in 2013, which is still operational today. In 2017, I decided to begin pursuing Software Development, as it had always been something of interest to me, and I felt the time was right to make a Career Change.
